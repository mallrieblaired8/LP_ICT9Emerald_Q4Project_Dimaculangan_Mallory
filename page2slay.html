CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Crisostomo Ibarra)
  • He is a richman who studied in Europe that came back to the Philippines after studying there for 7 years
  • He is the son of Don Rafael and Maria Clara's fiancée
María Clara de los Santos y Alba
  • She is the childhood friend and fiancée on Ibarra
  • She is the daughter of Capitan Tiago
  • She is the biological daughter of Damaso
Padre Dámaso Verdolagas
  • He is the old curate in San Diego
  • He is the godfather and biological father of Maria Clara
Elias
  • He is a fugitive living in San Diego
  • He is a patriotic leader of the insurrectos in the mountains and a friend of Baltazar.
  • He is Ibarra's close friend and helped him to express his goals for the Filipinos.
Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • He was the dictatator husband of Dona Victorina
  • A Spaniard who speaks with a stutter and looks significantly older than his 35 years
  • He came to the Philippines as a customs officer, but was dismissed upon his arrival
Doña Victorina de los Reyes de de Espadaña
  • She is a socialite and is one of the friends of Capitan Tiago
  • She is the wife of Don Tiburcio
Sisa
  • She is the deranged mother of Basilio and Crispín
  • She is a wife to an abusive man, Pedro
Crispin
  • He is the youngest son of Sisa and Pedro
  • He is one of the Sacristans in the church in San Diego
Basilio
  • He is the oldest son of Sisa and Pedro
  • He is also one of the Sacristans in the church in San Diego alongside his brother, Crispin
Tiya Isabel
  • She is the aunt and Maria Clara's caretaker and the cousin of Kapitan Tiago.
  • She is the head housekeeper in the De Los Santos homestead
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is considered the richest man in San Diego
  • He is the father of Maria Clara
Padre Salvi
  • He is the current town currate in San Diego
  • He is the successor of Padre Damaso
Don Rafael
  • He is the father of Ibarra
  • He was the one ordered to be imprisoned by Damaso
Alperes
  • He is the husband of Donyo Consolacion
  • he is the rival of Padre Salvi
Donya Consolacion
  • She is the one who married Alperes for money
  • She tries hard to speak Spanish because it will make her seem "better" than others
Pilosopo Tasio
  • They call him "baliw"
  • He had a lot of passion and love for Philosophy and is very intelligent
